Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about our pricing and fees

What is the 1% spread fee?

The 1% spread is included in the buy/sell price you see. When you buy, you pay 1% more than the market rate. When you sell, you receive 1% less than the market rate. This covers our operational costs and ensures fair pricing.

Is the ₹50 SIP fee negotiable?

The ₹50 monthly SIP fee is fixed and covers the automation, monitoring, and management of your systematic investment plan. Volume discounts may apply for large SIP amounts - contact support for details.

When are swap fees charged?

Swap fees are charged when you exchange one asset for another (e.g., Gold to Silver). The fee depends on the assets being swapped: 0.1% for BINR swaps, 0.3% for metal-to-metal swaps, and 0.5% for complex cross-asset swaps.

Are there any hidden charges?

No hidden charges ever. Our pricing is completely transparent. Account creation, maintenance, and portfolio holding are free. You only pay for actual transactions and SIP management.

Can I avoid trading fees?

Trading fees are standard across all platforms. However, you can minimize fees by using SIP for regular investments and holding assets long-term rather than frequent trading.

How is the market rate determined?

We use real-time feeds from major precious metals exchanges and banks. Our rates are updated every 30 seconds and are always competitive with prevailing market rates.
